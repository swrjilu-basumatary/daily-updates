Yamaha FZ-X Reviews 2024.

The Yamaha FZ-X is a modern yet retro-styled motorcycle, blending classic aesthetics with contemporary features, designed to cater to urban riders who seek a blend of style, comfort, and performance. Hereâ€™s an in-depth review

Design and Styling:

The Yamaha FZ-X showcases a neo-retro design inspired by vintage scramblers. It features a muscular fuel tank, a round LED headlamp with a daytime running light, a tuck-and-roll seat, and chunky dual-purpose tires. The rugged look is enhanced by fork gaiters, a metal bash plate, and a sturdy build, making it stand out in the urban jungle.



Engine and Performance:

The FZ-X is powered by a 149cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled, fuel-injected engine. This engine delivers a maximum power of 12.4 PS at 7,250 RPM and a peak torque of 13.3 Nm at 5,500 RPM. The engine is mated to a 5-speed gearbox, providing smooth and reliable performance for city commuting and occasional highway rides. While it might not be the most powerful in its segment, the engine offers adequate power for urban riding, focusing more on fuel efficiency and smooth delivery.



Comfort and Ergonomics:

Yamaha has designed the FZ-X with rider comfort in mind. The upright riding position, wide handlebars, and well-cushioned seat ensure a comfortable ride, even on longer journeys. The suspension setup includes telescopic forks at the front and a mono-shock at the rear, effectively absorbing road imperfections and providing a stable ride.



Features:

The Yamaha FZ-X is equipped with a fully digital LCD instrument cluster, Bluetooth connectivity for smartphone pairing, and Yamaha's proprietary Y-Connect app, which offers features like call and message alerts, fuel consumption tracking, and maintenance recommendations. Additionally, it comes with a side-stand engine cut-off switch and a single-channel ABS for enhanced safety.

Fuel Efficiency:

The FZ-X is known for its impressive fuel efficiency, with an average mileage of around 45-50 km/l, making it an economical choice for daily commuting.

Price and Variants:

Priced competitively in the 1.25 lakh to 1.30 lakh INR range (ex-showroom), the Yamaha FZ-X offers good value for money with its unique styling, decent performance, and practical features.

Conclusion

The Yamaha FZ-X is a compelling option for those looking for a stylish, comfortable, and feature-rich urban motorcycle. While it may not be the sportiest in its class, its retro charm, reliable performance, and modern amenities make it a strong contender in the 150cc segment.
